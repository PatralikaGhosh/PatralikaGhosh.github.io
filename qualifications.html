Technical Areas of Interest

Cybersecurity offers a wide range of career paths and specializations.
You can choose to be a cybersecurity analyst, penetration tester, ethical hacker, security consultant,
incident responder, or even work in management or policy development related to cybersecurity. Working in
cybersecurity allows you to play a critical role in protecting individuals, organizations, and even nations
from cyber threats. Your efforts can directly contribute to safeguarding sensitive information
and critical infrastructure.

Data science is at the forefront of innovation. You'll have the chance to work on cutting-edge
technologies like artificial intelligence (AI), deep learning, and natural language processing (NLP),
contributing to the development of groundbreaking solutions and products. Data science has the potential
to drive significant positive change. Whether you're improving healthcare outcomes, optimizing supply chains,
or enhancing customer experiences, your work can have a tangible impact on society and business.
